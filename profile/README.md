We are Computerization, a high school student club focusing on on-campus informatization systems in Shanghai World Foreign Language Academy.

## What we do

**Web:** Front-end development, website maintenance, and joint-club activities.  
**AI:** ML & DL, and exploring the latest technologies.  
**OI:** Contest preparation and algorithm lectures.

3. **[Outspire](https://github.com/Computerization/Outspire)** - All-in-one Campus App for WFLA.
